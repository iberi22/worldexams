# Question
What are the three branches of the U.S. federal government?

# Options
- [x] A) Legislative, Executive, and Judicial
- [ ] B) Federal, State, and Local
- [ ] C) Senate, House, and President
- [ ] D) Democratic, Republican, and Independent

# Explanation
The U.S. Constitution establishes three branches: Legislative (Congress), Executive (President), and Judicial (Supreme Court). This separation of powers prevents any one branch from becoming too powerful.
- B) These are levels of government, not branches
- C) Senate and House are parts of the Legislative branch
- D) These are political parties
