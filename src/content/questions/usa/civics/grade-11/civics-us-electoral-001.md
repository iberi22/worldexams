# Question
How many electoral votes are needed to win the U.S. presidential election?

# Options
- [ ] A) 218
- [x] B) 270
- [ ] C) 300
- [ ] D) 538

# Explanation
A candidate needs 270 electoral votes (a majority of the 538 total) to win the presidency. The total of 538 equals 435 Representatives + 100 Senators + 3 for Washington D.C.
- A) 218: Majority in the House of Representatives
- C) 300: More than required
- D) 538: Total electoral votes, not winning number
