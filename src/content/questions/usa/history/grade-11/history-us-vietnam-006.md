# Question
Which U.S. President oversaw the final withdrawal of American forces from South Vietnam?

# Options
- [ ] A) Lyndon B. Johnson
- [x] B) Richard Nixon
- [ ] C) Gerald Ford
- [ ] D) Jimmy Carter

# Explanation
Richard Nixon implemented the policy of "Vietnamization" and oversaw the withdrawal of U.S. combat forces from Vietnam. The Paris Peace Accords were signed in January 1973 during Nixon's presidency, officially ending direct U.S. military involvement. However, Gerald Ford was president when Saigon fell in April 1975, but by then U.S. forces had already withdrawn.
- A) Lyndon B. Johnson: Escalated U.S. involvement but withdrew from 1968 election
- C) Gerald Ford: President during the Fall of Saigon but after troop withdrawal
- D) Jimmy Carter: Became president in 1977, after the war ended
