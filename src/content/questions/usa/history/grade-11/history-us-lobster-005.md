# Question
How did the Lobster War relate to broader Cold War dynamics in Latin America?

# Options
- [ ] A) It was directly orchestrated by the Soviet Union
- [x] B) It reflected tensions between Western powers and Latin American nationalism during the Cold War
- [ ] C) It had no connection to Cold War politics
- [ ] D) It was part of the Cuban Missile Crisis

# Explanation
The Lobster War occurred during a period of heightened Cold War tensions in Latin America. Brazil's assertion of sovereignty over its maritime resources reflected growing Latin American nationalism and resistance to perceived European neo-colonialism. The U.S. monitored such conflicts carefully, concerned about communist influence in the region.
- A) Soviet Union: Not directly involved in orchestrating this dispute
- C) No connection: Incorrect; it was part of broader Cold War dynamics
- D) Cuban Missile Crisis: Separate event, though contemporaneous
