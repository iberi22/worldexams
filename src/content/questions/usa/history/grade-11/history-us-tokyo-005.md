# Question
The Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack occurred during which U.S. presidential administration?

# Options
- [ ] A) George H.W. Bush
- [x] B) Bill Clinton
- [ ] C) George W. Bush
- [ ] D) Barack Obama

# Explanation
The Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack occurred in 1995 during Bill Clinton's presidency (1993-2001). This event, along with the Oklahoma City bombing the same year, prompted the Clinton administration to increase focus on domestic and international terrorism preparedness, leading to significant counterterrorism legislation and policy changes.
- A) George H.W. Bush: President from 1989-1993, before the attack
- C) George W. Bush: President from 2001-2009, after the attack
- D) Barack Obama: President from 2009-2017, well after the attack
