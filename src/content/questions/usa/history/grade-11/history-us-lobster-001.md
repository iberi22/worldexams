# Question
Which countries participated in the Lobster War?

# Options
- [ ] A) Canada and Norway
- [ ] B) Australia and New Zealand
- [ ] C) United States and England
- [x] D) France and Brazil

# Explanation
The Lobster War (1961-1963) was a conflict between France and Brazil over lobster fishing rights off the Brazilian coast. The dispute centered on whether lobsters "walk" on the continental shelf (Brazil's position) or "swim" in international waters (France's position). This demonstrated Cold War-era tensions even over seemingly minor maritime disputes.
- A) Canada and Norway: Not involved in the Lobster War
- B) Australia and New Zealand: Not involved in this conflict
- C) United States and England: Not the primary participants
