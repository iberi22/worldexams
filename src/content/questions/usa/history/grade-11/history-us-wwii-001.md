# Question
What event caused the United States to enter World War II?

# Options
- [ ] A) Sinking of the Lusitania
- [ ] B) Invasion of Poland
- [x] C) Attack on Pearl Harbor
- [ ] D) D-Day invasion

# Explanation
The Japanese attack on Pearl Harbor, Hawaii, on December 7, 1941, led the United States to declare war on Japan and enter World War II.
- A) Lusitania: WWI event (1915)
- B) Poland invasion: Started WWII but US was neutral
- D) D-Day: 1944, US already in the war
