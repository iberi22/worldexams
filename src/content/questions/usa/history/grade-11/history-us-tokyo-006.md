# Question
What significant impact did the 1995 Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack have on U.S. security policy?

# Options
- [ ] A) It led to the creation of the TSA
- [x] B) It heightened awareness of chemical weapons terrorism and influenced WMD counterterrorism programs
- [ ] C) It resulted in the Patriot Act
- [ ] D) It created the Department of Homeland Security

# Explanation
The Tokyo Subway Sarin Attack demonstrated that non-state actors could acquire and use weapons of mass destruction (WMD), particularly chemical weapons. This influenced U.S. policy by strengthening programs to prevent WMD proliferation and enhancing preparedness for chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear (CBRN) terrorism threats.
- A) TSA was created after 9/11 in 2001, not after this attack
- C) Patriot Act was passed after 9/11 in 2001
- D) DHS was created in 2002 following 9/11
