# Question
When did the Fall of Saigon occur, marking the end of the Vietnam War?

# Options
- [ ] A) April 30, 1973
- [x] B) April 30, 1975
- [ ] C) April 30, 1976
- [ ] D) April 30, 1977

# Explanation
The Fall of Saigon occurred on April 30, 1975, when North Vietnamese forces captured the city, ending the Vietnam War. This event featured the iconic images of U.S. helicopters evacuating personnel from the U.S. Embassy rooftop. The fall symbolized the failure of U.S. containment policy in Southeast Asia and had profound effects on American foreign policy and national psychology.
- A) 1973: Year of Paris Peace Accords and U.S. troop withdrawal
- C) 1976: Year after the fall
- D) 1977: Two years after the fall
