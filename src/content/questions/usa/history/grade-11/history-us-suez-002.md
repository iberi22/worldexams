# Question
In which year was the Suez Canal officially opened?

# Options
- [ ] A) 1859
- [x] B) 1869
- [ ] C) 1879
- [ ] D) 1889

# Explanation
The Suez Canal was officially opened on November 17, 1869, after ten years of construction. The canal revolutionized global trade by eliminating the need to sail around Africa. Its opening marked a significant milestone in the age of imperialism and had lasting implications for international trade and geopolitics, including U.S. commercial interests.
- A) 1859: Construction began in 1859, but opening was a decade later
- C) 1879: Ten years after the actual opening
- D) 1889: Twenty years after the opening
