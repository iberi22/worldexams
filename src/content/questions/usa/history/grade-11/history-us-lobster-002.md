# Question
During which period did the Lobster War between France and Brazil take place?

# Options
- [ ] A) 1955-1957
- [x] B) 1961-1963
- [ ] C) 1968-1970
- [ ] D) 1973-1975

# Explanation
The Lobster War occurred from 1961 to 1963, during the early Cold War period. This maritime dispute coincided with heightened tensions in Latin America, including the Cuban Missile Crisis (1962). The conflict was resolved diplomatically, with both nations eventually reaching an agreement on fishing rights.
- A) 1955-1957: Too early; the conflict hadn't begun
- C) 1968-1970: Too late; the dispute was already resolved
- D) 1973-1975: Well after the conflict ended
