# Question
Which two bodies of water does the Suez Canal connect?

# Options
- [ ] A) Atlantic Ocean and Pacific Ocean
- [x] B) Mediterranean Sea and Red Sea
- [ ] C) Persian Gulf and Arabian Sea
- [ ] D) Black Sea and Caspian Sea

# Explanation
The Suez Canal connects the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea, providing a direct maritime route between Europe and Asia without requiring ships to navigate around Africa. This 120-mile waterway reduced the journey from London to India by approximately 7,000 miles, transforming global trade patterns and influencing U.S. commercial expansion in the late 19th century.
- A) Atlantic-Pacific: The Panama Canal connects these oceans
- C) Persian Gulf-Arabian Sea: Not connected by the Suez Canal
- D) Black Sea-Caspian Sea: These are not connected by the Suez Canal
