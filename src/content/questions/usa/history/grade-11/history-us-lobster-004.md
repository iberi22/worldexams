# Question
Which Brazilian president was in office during the Lobster War with France?

# Options
- [ ] A) Getúlio Vargas
- [x] B) João Goulart
- [ ] C) Juscelino Kubitschek
- [ ] D) Fernando Henrique Cardoso

# Explanation
João Goulart was Brazil's president (1961-1964) during the Lobster War. His leftist policies and confrontation with France over fishing rights reflected broader Cold War tensions in Latin America. The U.S. closely monitored the situation, concerned about communist influence in the region during this period.
- A) Getúlio Vargas: Earlier president who died in 1954
- C) Juscelino Kubitschek: President from 1956-1961, before the Lobster War
- D) Fernando Henrique Cardoso: President from 1995-2003, decades later
