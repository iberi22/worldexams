# Question
Which U.S. military operation evacuated Americans and South Vietnamese allies from Saigon in April 1975?

# Options
- [ ] A) Operation Rolling Thunder
- [ ] B) Operation Linebacker
- [x] C) Operation Frequent Wind
- [ ] D) Operation Ranch Hand

# Explanation
Operation Frequent Wind was the final U.S. military operation in Vietnam, evacuating over 7,000 people by helicopter from Saigon on April 29-30, 1975. The chaotic evacuation, with helicopters lifting off from embassy rooftops and desperate civilians seeking escape, became one of the most powerful images of American military defeat and the human cost of the Vietnam War.
