# Question
What major Cold War crisis involved the Suez Canal in 1956?

# Options
- [ ] A) The Berlin Blockade
- [ ] B) The Cuban Missile Crisis
- [x] C) The Suez Crisis
- [ ] D) The Korean War

# Explanation
The Suez Crisis of 1956 occurred when Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser nationalized the Suez Canal, leading to military intervention by Britain, France, and Israel. President Eisenhower opposed this action and pressured the aggressors to withdraw, marking a shift in global power from European colonial powers to the U.S. and Soviet Union as superpowers.
- A) Berlin Blockade: Occurred 1948-1949, different crisis
- B) Cuban Missile Crisis: Occurred in 1962, unrelated to Suez
- D) Korean War: Occurred 1950-1953, separate conflict
