# Question
Who was the main developer of the Suez Canal?

# Options
- [x] A) Ferdinand de Lesseps
- [ ] B) Louis-Eugène Cavaignac
- [ ] C) John Milton
- [ ] D) Johann Lukas von Hildebrandt

# Explanation
Ferdinand de Lesseps was the French diplomat and engineer who developed the Suez Canal, which opened in 1869. The canal connected the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea, dramatically reducing travel time between Europe and Asia. This project exemplified European imperial ambitions and technological prowess during the 19th century.
- B) Louis-Eugène Cavaignac: French general and politician, not associated with the canal
- C) John Milton: English poet from the 17th century
- D) Johann Lukas von Hildebrandt: Austrian architect, not related to the canal
