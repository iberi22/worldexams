# Question
Who delivered the famous "I Have a Dream" speech during the March on Washington in 1963?

# Options
- [x] A) Martin Luther King Jr.
- [ ] B) Malcolm X
- [ ] C) Rosa Parks
- [ ] D) John F. Kennedy

# Explanation

- C) Rosa Parks: Sparked Montgomery Bus Boycott
- D) JFK: President who supported civil rights legislation
