# Question
What chemical weapon was used in the 1995 Tokyo Subway Attack that influenced U.S. counterterrorism policy?

# Options
- [ ] A) Mustard gas
- [x] B) Sarin nerve gas
- [ ] C) Chlorine gas
- [ ] D) VX nerve agent

# Explanation
Sarin nerve gas was used in the 1995 Tokyo Subway Attack. This attack demonstrated that terrorist groups could acquire and deploy chemical weapons, leading the U.S. to enhance its counterterrorism measures and chemical weapons monitoring programs. Sarin is an organophosphate nerve agent that disrupts the nervous system.
- A) Mustard gas: Used in World War I, causes blistering
- C) Chlorine gas: Less lethal than sarin, also from WWI
- D) VX nerve agent: Different nerve agent, not used in this attack
