# Question
In what year was the Apple I personal computer released?

# Options
- [ ] A) 1974
- [x] B) 1976
- [ ] C) 1978
- [ ] D) 1980

# Explanation
The Apple I was released in 1976 by Steve Wozniak and Steve Jobs. It was Apple's first product and one of the first personal computers on the market. About 200 units were produced.
- A) 1974: This is too early; Apple Computer Company wasn't founded until 1976
- C) 1978: This is when the Apple II was gaining popularity
- D) 1980: This is when the Apple III was released
