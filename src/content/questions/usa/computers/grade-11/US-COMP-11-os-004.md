# Question
Which Ubuntu release came first chronologically?

# Options
- [x] A) Trusty Tahr
- [ ] B) Utopic Unicorn
- [ ] C) Wily Werewolf
- [ ] D) Xenial Xerus

# Explanation
Trusty Tahr (14.04) was released in April 2014, making it the earliest among these options. The releases follow alphabetical order: Trusty Tahr (T), Utopic Unicorn (U), Wily Werewolf (W), and Xenial Xerus (X).
- B) Utopic Unicorn (14.10) came after Trusty Tahr
- C) Wily Werewolf (15.10) came after both T and U releases
- D) Xenial Xerus (16.04) came after all the others listed
