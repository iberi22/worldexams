# Question
Who was the primary designer of the Apple I computer?

# Options
- [x] A) Steve Wozniak
- [ ] B) Steve Jobs
- [ ] C) Ronald Wayne
- [ ] D) Mike Markkula

# Explanation
Steve Wozniak was the primary designer and engineer who created the Apple I computer. Steve Jobs focused on marketing and selling the computers, while Wozniak handled the technical design. Together they produced about 200 units in 1976.
- B) Steve Jobs was the business partner who marketed the Apple I
- C) Ronald Wayne was a co-founder but left Apple early on
- D) Mike Markkula was an early investor who joined later
