## Pregunta 1 (Original - Dificultad Medium)

**ID:** "CO-ING-11-reading-002-v1"

### Enunciado

What does Maria do after school?

### Opciones

- [ ] A) She wakes up
- [ ] B) She walks to school
- [x] C) She does homework and plays with friends
- [ ] D) She has breakfast

### Explicación Pedagógica

**¿Por qué C es correcta?**
El texto indica la secuencia temporal: "**After school**, she goes home, does her homework, and plays soccer...".

**Competencia evaluada:** Lectura Literal - Identificación de Secuencia
**Componente:** Reading Comprehension

## Pregunta 7 (High B - Dificultad High)

**ID:** "CO-ING-11-reading-002-v7"

### Enunciado

In the sentence "She has a big breakfast with **her** family", the word "**her**" refers to:

### Opciones

- [x] A) Maria's
- [ ] B) The family's
- [ ] C) The school's
- [ ] D) The friend's

### Explicación Pedagógica

**¿Por qué A es correcta?**
El pronombre posesivo "her" se refiere al sujeto de la oración y del texto, que es Maria. Significa "la familia de Maria".

**Competencia evaluada:** Referencia Pronominal
**Componente:** Grammar in Context
