# Pregunta
What is the opposite of "expensive"?

# Opciones
- [ ] A) Costly
- [x] B) Cheap
- [ ] C) Rich
- [ ] D) Beautiful

# Explicación
"Cheap" means low in price, which is the opposite of "expensive" (high in price).
