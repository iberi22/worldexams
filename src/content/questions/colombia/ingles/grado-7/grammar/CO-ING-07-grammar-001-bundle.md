## Pregunta 2 (Low A - Dificultad Low)

**ID:** `CO-ING-07-grammar-001-v2`

### Question

Complete the sentence:
**I ______ soccer every Sunday.**

### Options

- [x] A) play
- [ ] B) plays
- [ ] C) playing
- [ ] D) played

### Explanation

For "I" (First person singular), the verb in Present Simple stays in its base form.
*   "I play" is correct.
*   "plays" is for He/She/It.

**Competency evaluated:** Grammar - Present Simple Affirmative.

## Pregunta 4 (Medium A - Dificultad Medium)

**ID:** `CO-ING-07-grammar-001-v4`

### Question

Choose the correct question format:

### Options

- [ ] A) Do she live in Bogota?
- [x] B) Does she live in Bogota?
- [ ] C) Does she lives in Bogota?
- [ ] D) Live she in Bogota?

### Explanation

Questions in Present Simple follow: **Auxiliary (Do/Does) + Subject + Verb (base)?**
*   For "She", we use "Does".
*   The main verb must be in base form ("live", not "lives").

**Competency evaluated:** Grammar - Interrogative Form.

## Pregunta 6 (High A - Dificultad High)

**ID:** `CO-ING-07-grammar-001-v6`

### Question

Choose the sentence with the correct **Frequency Adverb** position:

### Options

- [ ] A) I go always to the park on Saturdays.
- [x] B) I always go to the park on Saturdays.
- [ ] C) Always I go to the park on Saturdays.
- [ ] D) I go to the park always on Saturdays.

### Explanation

Frequency adverbs (always, never, usually) generally go **before the main verb** (except with verb To Be).
*   Correct: "I **always go**..."
*   Incorrect: "I go always..."

**Competency evaluated:** Grammar - Word Order / Adverbs.
